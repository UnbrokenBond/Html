Our Story

My husband and I would consider ourselves the black sheep of our families. Both a little edgy and different but have always shown that with our style. However, coming to embrace my style was not always easy. I saw everything so negatively and it made me scared to be who I wanted to be. Eventually I forced myself out of my shell and decided I wanted better and explore the endless stylistic possibilities. When you come into the shop you'll see me with a new dew essentially everytime! This was my journey to where I am today and I want every person that sits in my chair to feel liberated to be themselves too.

Our Vision

Your hair is part of your identity! So getting that perfect blowout, haircut, or color will reflect your truest happiest self. Hair is my favorite way to bring positivity into my life and others. And hopefully yours someday too!
